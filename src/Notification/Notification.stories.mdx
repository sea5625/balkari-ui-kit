# Notification

The `Notification` are messages that communicate information to the user. The two main types of notifications are toast notifications and inline notifications.

---

## Style

---

### Default Notification (Inline, Toast)

---

<Notification
    title="Default Inline Notification Title"
    subTitle="Default Inline Notification SubTitle."
    kind="primary"
    maxWidth="38rem"
/>

---

<Notification
    title="Default Toast Message Title"
    subTitle="Default Toast Message SubTitle."
    kind="primary"
    type="toast"
    caption="Toast Message captions"
/>

---

1. Icon: Informs users of the type of notification at a glance.
2. Title: Gives users a quick overview of the notification.
3. Subtitle (optional): Provides additional detail and actionable steps for the user to take.
4. Close button (optional): Closes the notification.
5. Caption (optional): Shows the time the toast notification was sent.

### Placement

Inline notifications appear near their related items. They can expand to fill the width of the container or content area they are in and should align to the grid columns.

We recommend placing inline notifications at the bottom of forms, just above the submission and cancel buttons. When error notifications apply to individual text inputs, they should supplement the error state on that specific input field

<TextInput
    value="passwordpassword"
    type="password"
    passwordVisibleButton
    passwordVisibleButton
    invalid
    invalidValue="Your Password must be more then 8 characters long"
/>

---

<Notification
    title="Password Entry Error"
    subTitle="Please check the password you entered."
    kind="error"
    maxWidth="100%"
    hideCloseButton
/>
